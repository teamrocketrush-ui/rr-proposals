Our process

How we work

All 5 phases run simultaneously from day one and compound over time — the longer we run, the stronger the network effect.

Phase 01
Story Architecture
A recorded conversation covering your origin stories, decisions, failures, beliefs, and POVs. This becomes the raw asset for all content — 2 hours every month. We define your brand guidelines: voice, boundaries, no-go zones, and Zone of Genius.
Phase 02
Content Engine Launch
Stories converted into repeatable narrative angles. Two weeks of posts shared 5 days before posting. First post goes live 10–12 working days after the story session. Edits turned around within 24–48 hours.
Phase 03
Audience Research
We document your customers' fears, frustrations, desires, and objections. Cross-team research and competitor analysis finds gaps you can own — so your content speaks your audience's language, not marketing jargon.
Phase 04
Cluster Penetration
We map communities where your people spend time — founders, CXOs, VCs, operators. Inside those clusters: like the right posts, comment thoughtfully, connect without pitching. Your name becomes familiar before they ever see your post.
Phase 05
The Network Effect
Because of Phases 3 and 4, something powerful starts happening. People follow after seeing your comments. Founders, CXOs, and operators start entering your inbox.

Visibility turns into relationships. Relationships turn into deals, speaking invites, and collaborations. All 5 phases working at once activate your entire network.
Metrics

What we measure

We go beyond vanity metrics. We track signals that indicate real business impact — specifically how many of your ideal customers send you connection requests, view your profile, DM you, comment on posts, or save and share your content.
